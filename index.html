01 / Problem

The drone data industry
is broken for Texas.

Enterprise tools charge subscription fees regardless of project volume. Data gets locked behind platforms. Clients can't view their own assets without a monthly bill. Texas land professionals deserve better.

01

Subscription Lock-In

DroneDeploy and competitors charge $500–$2,000/month. A homeowner with one parcel pays the same as a developer with 200 sites.

02

No Time Intelligence

Existing platforms show a single snapshot. No one has built time-based land visualization that shows how a property evolves across captures.

03

Global Tools, Local Blindness

No platform is built specifically for Texas datasets — NAD83/NAD27 coordinate systems, county parcel data, and Texas-specific land use patterns.

04

Fragmented Delivery

Pilots deliver via Dropbox. Clients receive raw files. There's no unified platform connecting capture, processing, and interactive delivery.

02 / Platform

A living map of
Texas land intelligence.

Land on a Texas base map. Click a project site. View orthomosaics, point clouds, and DEMs as interactive layers. Scrub through time if multiple captures exist. Every deliverable is geospatially anchored, auth-gated, and yours permanently.

03 / Edge

Not another SaaS tool.
A new category.

🗺️

Texas-First Precision

NAD83/NAD27 coordinate support, Texas county parcel data, hard state boundary enforcement. Built for how Texas land professionals actually work.

⏱️

Time-Based Visualization

The only platform with a multi-capture timeline slider. Watch land develop, erode, flood, or grow across months and years of drone data.

💳

Per-Project Pricing

Pay once per project. View forever. No subscription required to access your own deliverables. A direct answer to the DroneDeploy problem.

🔒

Private by Default

All data served through auth-gated tile endpoints. No direct file URLs. No third-party data access. Client data stays yours.

🚁

Integrated Marketplace

Clients can hire vetted Texas pilots directly. Pilots upload captures to the platform. The full pipeline — capture to delivery — in one place.

📡

Self-Hosted Infrastructure

No third-party cloud lock-in. All data stored on controlled infrastructure. Costs scale with storage, not with seat count or project volume.
